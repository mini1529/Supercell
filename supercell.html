Supercell

Supercell is a mobile game developer based in Helsinki, Finland, with offices in San Francisco, Seoul and Shanghai.
Since our launch in 2010, we've brought five games to the global market.
Hay Day, Clash of Clans, Boom Beach, Clash Royale and Brawl Stars.
Our goal is to make the best games - games that are played by as many people as possible, enjoyed for years and remembered forever.
